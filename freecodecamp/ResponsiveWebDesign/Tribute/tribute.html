Strange Facts

  • The FBI monitored Hemingway during World War II because he was a suspected spy for the Soviet Union.
  • National newspapers mistakenly reported that Hemingway was dead after he survived two plane crashes in two days.
  • Hemingway owned a six-toed cat whose descendants today reside in his former home in Key West, Florida.
